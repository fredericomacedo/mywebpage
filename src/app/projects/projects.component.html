Nokia Internal Project Cost Management System

For Nokia, I spearheaded a full-stack web application complemented by a comprehensive business analysis solution, focusing on streamlined internal project cost management. This centralized system offers real-time expenditure tracking through intuitive interfaces developed with CSS, JavaScript, and HTTP. PowerFX facilitated dynamic data interactions, while SharePoint provided organized data storage. Power Automator, seamlessly integrated, automated critical tasks like data imports and notifications, ensuring both efficiency and precision. Securely hosted on Azure Environment, the application ensures scalability and consistent performance. My holistic approach not only digitalized Nokia's cost management but also empowered decision-makers with immediate, accurate insights, thereby optimizing project budget allocation and overall business strategy. May 2023.
